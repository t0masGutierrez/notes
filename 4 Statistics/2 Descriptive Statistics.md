### empirical rule
- 68% of normal data within 1 standard deviation
- 95% of normal data within 2 standard deviation
- 99.7% of normal data within 3 standard deviation
---
### empirical rule of thumb
- two standard deviation equal significant deviation

---
### outlier
- significant deviation from the mean

---
### lower outlier formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
x = \text{Q1} - 1.5 \times \text{IQR} \\
\text{Q1} = \text{1st quartile} \\
\text{IQR} = \text{interquartile range}
\end{aligned}
$$

---
### upper outlier formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
x = \text{Q3} + 1.5 \times \text{IQR} \\
\text{Q3} = \text{3rd quartile} \\
\text{IQR} = \text{interquartile range}
\end{aligned}
$$
