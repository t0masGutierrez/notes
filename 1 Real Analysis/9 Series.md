### pointwise
- parameter dependent point

---
### pointwise formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
\forall x, \exists p: P(x, p) \\
x = \text{point} \\
p = \text{parameter} \\
P = \text{proposition}
\end{aligned}
$$

---
### pointwise convergent
- for every point there exists there exists limit of sequence

---
### pointwise convergent formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
\forall x \in S, \exists f \in \mathbb R: \lim_{n \rightarrow \infty} f_{n}(x) = f(x) \\
f_{n} : S \rightarrow \mathbb R \\
f = \text{sequential limit} \\
\set{f_{n}} = \text{convergent sequence} 
\end{aligned}
$$

---
### series
- sum of terms of sequence

---
### series formula
$$
\begin{aligned}
\forall x \in S, \exists f \in \mathbb R: \sum_{n=1}^\infty f_{n}(x) = f(x) \\
f_{n} : S \rightarrow \mathbb R \\
f = \text{sum} \\
\sum f_{n} = \text{series} 
\end{aligned}
$$
